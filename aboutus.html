About Us

Ore Grihobashi was born out of the shared dreams of four tech enthusiasts and lifelong friends- Atreyee Das, Ankit Talukder, Shreesh Chakraborty, and Archisman Kundu. Growing up together in the heart of Bengal, from school to college, we always envisioned making West Bengal a prominent destination on the global tourism map. As proud Bengalis, we realized that the beauty, history, and cultural heritage of our homeland deserved to be explored and cherished by all.
Driven by our love for technology and our passion for Bengal's rich heritage, we created Ore Grihobashi to make exploring West Bengal both easy and affordable. Our platform not only opens doors for travelers to experience the state's unique traditions but also ensures the safety and comfort of every journey. By preserving the essence of Bengal's history and culture, we aim to transform the region into a renowned tourist attraction while honoring its timeless legacy.
